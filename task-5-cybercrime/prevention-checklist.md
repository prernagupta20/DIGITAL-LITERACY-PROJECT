## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist 

###  General Safety Tips
- Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading files from unknown sources.  
- Keep your social media profiles private and limit sharing of personal information.  
- Verify the authenticity of emails, messages, or calls before responding.  
- Do not share OTPs, passwords, or personal details with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank or official organization.  
- Regularly update your apps and devices to protect against security vulnerabilities.  

###  Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- Never approve unknown “collect requests” on UPI apps; receiving money does not require entering your UPI PIN.  
- Double-check the recipient’s details before making any payment and avoid scanning QR codes from untrusted sources.  
- Do not share your UPI PIN or banking details with anyone under any circumstances.  

###  Reporting Cybercrime
- Report cybercrime incidents at the **National Cyber Crime Portal**: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- For immediate assistance, call the **Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930**  

---

Staying informed and cautious online is the key to protecting your data, finances, and digital identity.
