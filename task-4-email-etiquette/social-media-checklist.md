# Social Media Do's and Dont's Checklist

##  Do’s
- Think carefully before posting any content and ensure it is appropriate and respectful.  
- Use strong, unique passwords and enable privacy and security settings.  
- Verify the authenticity of information before sharing it.  
- Maintain respectful and professional communication with others.  
- Manage screen time effectively and prioritize academic responsibilities.  

##  Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.  
- Do not engage in cyberbullying, harassment, or offensive behavior.  
- Do not accept friend requests or messages from unknown individuals.  
- Do not spread misinformation or unverified content.  
- Do not overshare your location or daily activities online.
